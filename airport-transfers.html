Transfer FAQ

Before you land

Can I book a late-night or early-morning pickup?

Yes. Send your arrival time and route early so the driver, vehicle type, and contact details can be confirmed before you travel.

What should I send for airport pickup?

Send arrival date, flight number, passenger count, luggage size, drop-off location, and the WhatsApp number we should use for updates.

Can you combine airport transfer with hotel booking?

Yes. Augur can coordinate your hotel, airport transfer, and onward tour or vehicle hire together for a smoother arrival plan.
