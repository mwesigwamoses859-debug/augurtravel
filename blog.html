Primate Travel 20 June 2026

Chimpanzee tracking or habituation in Kibale?

Chimpanzee tracking is designed around a focused forest encounter. Under current UWA guidance, the habituation experience can allow up to four hours of viewing after the chimpanzees are located, offering a deeper look at behavior, research, and the rhythm of the forest.

Plan early because permits and group sizes are controlled. Kibale also combines naturally with crater lakes, Queen Elizabeth National Park, and longer western Uganda circuits.

Safari Ideas 15 June 2026

Beyond the classic game drive

Murchison Falls can include Nile bird watching, sport fishing, and an authorized night game drive in addition to the falls and daytime wildlife routes. Queen Elizabeth adds the Kazinga Channel boat ride and the forested Kyambura Gorge.

Special activities depend on permits, park rules, season, and availability, so they should be requested while the itinerary is being built.

Mountain Travel 10 June 2026

Rwenzori or Mount Elgon: choosing your mountain journey

The Rwenzori range suits travelers looking for dramatic high-altitude scenery, multi-day trekking, or a technical summit plan. Mount Elgon offers varied forest trails, a broad volcanic landscape, waterfalls, caves, and shorter Sipi-based experiences.

Your route should match fitness, available days, season, equipment, and comfort level. Augur can arrange transport and connect mountain plans with other regions.

Conservation 4 June 2026

Why Ziwa fits naturally into a Murchison itinerary

Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary gives travelers a guided on-foot rhino tracking experience and a chance to learn about rhino conservation in Uganda. Its location makes it a practical stop on a northern circuit.

A well-timed visit can be combined with Murchison Falls without turning the route into a rushed checklist.

Cross-border Travel 29 May 2026

Adding Rwanda to a Uganda safari

A Rwanda extension can include Kigali, Volcanoes National Park, Akagera, or a relaxing Lake Kivu stay. It works especially well with south-western Uganda routes when border timing, permits, and transport are planned together.

Share your passport nationality, preferred dates, interests, and entry or departure airport so the route can be checked properly.
