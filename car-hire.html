Car Hire FAQ

What to include in your request

What details help you quote faster?

Share pickup point, destination, travel dates, number of passengers, luggage size, preferred vehicle type, and whether you need a driver.

Can I request a car for safari routes?

Yes. Tell us the parks or regions you plan to visit so we can recommend a vehicle suitable for road conditions and luggage needs.

Do you support corporate or NGO transport?

Yes. Augur can help with staff movement, field transport, conference shuttles, and longer hire arrangements for teams.
