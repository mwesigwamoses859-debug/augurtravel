Hotel FAQ

Booking enquiry checklist

What should I include in a hotel request?

Send destination, check-in and check-out dates, number of rooms, guest count, budget guide, room style, and any must-have amenities.

Can you book safari lodges?

Yes. Share the parks or activities you want, and we can coordinate lodge options with route planning, vehicles, and guide support.

Can hotel booking be combined with transfers?

Yes. Airport pickups, city transfers, vehicle hire, and tour planning can be bundled with your accommodation request.
